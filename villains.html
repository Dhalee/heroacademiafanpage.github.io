Villains of MHA

The antagonists in My Hero Academia who challenge hero society.

League of Villains

The main antagonistic group led by Tomura Shigaraki.

  • Tomura Shigaraki: Leader with Decay quirk
  • Dabi: Fire user with mysterious past
  • Himiko Toga: Blood-based transformation
  • Twice: Can create clones
  • Mr. Compress: Can compress objects

All For One

The master villain who can steal and give quirks. Former mentor of All Might.

Quirk: All For One - Can steal and stockpile quirks from others.
