My Hero Academia is a Japanese superhero manga series written and illustrated by Kohei Horikoshi. In a world where 80% of the population has superpowers called "Quirks", follow the journey of Izuku Midoriya as he trains to become the greatest hero!

Izuku "Deku" Midoriya

The main protagonist of My Hero Academia. Initially Quirkless, he inherits "One For All" from All Might and enrolls in UA High School to become a professional hero.

Quirk: One For All

Hero Name: Deku

Katsuki Bakugo

Deku's childhood friend and rival. Known for his explosive personality and powerful Quirk, he aims to become the number one hero.

Quirk: Explosion

Hero Name: Great Explosion Murder God Dynamight
